General Rules

  • Clear Instructions & References are required.
  • Prices can vary based on type of work.
  • Only accept payment via UPI or Bank transfer.
  • You can pay 100% upfront or split 50% upfront and 50% before delivery.
  • Don't send me any payment before i have agreed to do the work or requested for payment.
  • The work will take usually 7-10 days (extra charges for early delivery).

Revisions

  • You get 3 rounds of changes on the commissioned piece included in the price.
  • A fee will be added if you want something changed on the final drawing - unless a misunderstanding from my side has been made.
  • If you wish for me to change something in the drawing you have previously approved, I will charge you a fee to change it. The amount of the extra charge depends on the change you want me to make and is usually based on an hourly wage.

Copyright & Usage-The Artist

  • I retain all copyrights over the commissioned artwork.
  • I will not claim the Intellectual Property(IP) of the artwork.
  • I will NOT profit any further from the commissioned artwork unless you(the customer) break any of the rules.
  • I reserve the rights to post the artwork online & showcase in my portfolio.

Copyright & Usage-The Customer

  • You retain the rights to Intellectual Property(IP) of the commissioned artwork.
  • You may upload the artwork to any website & social media.
  • You may NOT resell or make any profit from the commissioned artwork. No Commercial use.
  • You may not alter the artwork without my permission.

Cancellation & Refund policy

  • You(the buyer) are not allowed to cancel the order once I've started the work.
  • If I am unable to start the work for any reason, you will get a full refund.
  • If you cancel your order before I started it, you can get a full refund.
